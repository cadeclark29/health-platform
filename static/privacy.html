Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

What We Collect

To provide personalized supplement recommendations, we collect:

  • Account information: Name, email, age, sex, and weight
  • Health data from Oura: Sleep scores, HRV, recovery metrics, and activity data
  • Supplement history: What you've dispensed and when
  • Lifestyle preferences: Diet type, activity level, and sleep schedule

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized supplement recommendations
  • Adjust dosages based on your health metrics
  • Track your supplement intake and saturation levels
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect your Oura account to stop health data sync

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove all your data within 30 days.

Questions? Contact us at privacy@dose.health
